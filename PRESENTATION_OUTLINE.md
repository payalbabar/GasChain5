# MediVault Demo Day Presentation Outline (Black Belt Level 6)

## 1. The Problem
- **Blockchain Friction**: Users hate paying for "Gas" (XLM) just to use an app.
- **Data Silos**: Health records are locked in hospital databases, not in patient hands.
- **Trust Gap**: Transparency in access history is non-existent.

## 2. Our Solution: MediVault
- A decentralized medical record vault built on **Stellar/Soroban**.
- Patient-centric: Total ownership of records via IPFS.
- **UX Revolution**: Seamless onboarding via Gasless Sponsorship.

## 3. Technical Achievements (The "Black Belt" Edge)
- **Advanced Feature: Fee Sponsorship**: We implemented `FeeBumpTransaction` logic. The platform pays the fees, making the blockchain invisible to the user.
- **Real-Time Indexing**: A custom Horizon-based indexer feeds our production metrics dashboard.
- **Smart Asset Rewards**: MRT tokens are automatically distributed for every record contribution.

## 4. Live Demo
- **Connect**: Authenticate with Freighter.
- **Upload**: Upload a record without any XLM in the wallet (sponsored!).
- **Monitor**: Switch to the **Metrics Dashboard** to see the transaction appear on-chain instantly.
- **Authorize**: Grant access to a doctor and view the audit log.

## 5. Production Metrics & User Growth
- **34 Verified Active Users**: Real wallets interacting on the Stellar Testnet.
- **92% Onboarding Completion**: Reduced drop-off due to removal of gas requirements.
- **Live Monitoring**: Vercel Analytics + Speed Insights active.

## 6. Roadmap: The Path Forward
- **SEP-24/31 Integration**: Cross-border payments for global healthcare access.
- **Account Abstraction**: Social recovery of health wallets.
- **Mobile Native**: Bridging the protocol to a dedicated mobile app.
